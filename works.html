Maestro's Adventure Game

Maestro's Adventure is a Python & Pygame-based game where players act as a maestro, catching correct music notes to create melodies while avoiding wrong notes that disrupt the harmony. Set in a music hall, the maestro must also dodge falling spotlights that threaten their performance, with only three lives to risk. The objective is to skillfully maintain the orchestra and create the perfect melody without losing all lives.
